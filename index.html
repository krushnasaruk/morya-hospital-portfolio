ABOUT US

Morya Multispeciality Hospital

Welcome to Morya Multispeciality Hospital, a trusted name in comprehensive medical care in Wadgaon Budruk, Pune. Our hospital is led by a team of skilled and experienced doctors, including Dr. Rashmi Patil and Dr. Vaibhav Patil, who are committed to delivering high-quality and personalized healthcare services. We specialize in Gynecology, Obstetrics, Internal Medicine, and General Practice, offering advanced treatments such as Laparoscopic Hysterectomy, Menstrual Disorder Management, Infertility Treatment, PCOD/PCOS Care, and Newborn Follow-Ups.

At Morya Multispeciality Hospital, Pune, we are equipped to provide a wide range of services, including Thyroid Surgery, Total Knee and Hip Replacement (TKR/THR), Arthroscopic Procedures, Kidney Stone Removal, UTI Treatment, and Post-Surgical Rehabilitation. Our focus is on delivering evidence-based care with compassion and precision for conditions ranging from common infections to complex surgeries. Explore our website to learn more about our facilities, access trustworthy medical information, and consult with our experts online. We aim to be your first choice for multispeciality care in Wadgaon Budruk, Pune, committed to your family's health and well-being.
